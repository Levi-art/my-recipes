Harmburger

Description

Hamburger is suggested to be invented by united states or germany, derived from the word 'hamburg

Ingredients

  • onions
  • beef
  • egg
  • white rolls
  • oil
  • mixed dried herbs

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate
  2. whisk egg,salt and pepper in a bowl
  3. form into four thick patties
  4. place patties on the preheated grill
  5. serve and enjoy
