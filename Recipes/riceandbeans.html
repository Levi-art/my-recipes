Rice and Beans

Description

Rice and beans is food commonly eaten by nigerians and its one of my favourite meal

It can be served as a main dish or side dish

Ingredients needed as follows

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pick the beans,rinse,and cook till tender for about 30 mins
  2. Rinse the rice a couple times till the water is clear and add the rice to the beans
  3. Add salt to taste
  4. Cook till done
  5. Serve with your favourite sauce or stew
